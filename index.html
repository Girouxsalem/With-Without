With/Without

A poem by Claude Giroux

Too much, Or not enough, As empty as a void, Or as full as a day when all you want to do is nothing, Living with death, Or dying with life, Is either a viable option?, Conclusions never reached, Have to think about my feet 'less I fall into a trap of my own doing, The mind is but a fly that is stuck between your eyes, It cannot see and it does not know where it's going.
